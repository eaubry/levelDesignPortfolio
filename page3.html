about me

My name is Evan Aubry. I'm 26, have a BA in Landscape Architecture, and have had fun designing a variety of different places for my job for the past 5 years. I love playing video games and designing cool spaces for people to experience (hence my job), so naturally I've become interested in combining the two.

Currently, I work as a level designer for an indie game studio, and despite not actively practicing landscape architecture anymore, the skills that I learned from that field seem to overlap almost every day- regardless of if it's about designing real places or levels in a video game, the process revolves around user experience and creating memorable adventures.

When I'm not designing games for my job, you can usually find me... uh, designing games for fun. In fact, I have a little side business (just starting out) that makes video games and board games. You can check it out here.
