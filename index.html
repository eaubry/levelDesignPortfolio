Hi. My name is Evan Aubry and I'm a game level designer. I enjoy coming up with ideas for puzzles, stories, interactions, and gameplay elements that bring enjoyment to those who play my games. Coming from a background as a landscape architect, designing spaces for people to visit and create memorable experiences has always been an interest of mine, and I feel this combines very well with my passion for games.

I'm work as part of an indie studio developing our first commercial game, and in addition, I have quite a few other projects of my own that I have finished or are still in the works. I currently have a mobile game on the Google PlayStore, a Pokemon ROM hack, and my own indie platformer that I plan to release for PC in the future.

With a Bachelor of Arts in Landscape Architecture, I'm no stranger to the world of design and have quite a history of professional spacial design. As for computer science and coding, that's all come from experience of working on indie games and pursuing my passion for gaming.

Feel free to take a look at some of the projects I've worked on.
